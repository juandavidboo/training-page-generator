Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend you start by reviewing our learning paths which lay out a step-by-step sequence of courses and certifications by role.

Each certification has preparation materials to help you practice and test your knowledge before sitting the exam. Select the certification you are looking to take from our catalog then review the “Preparation” section.

Completion of the self-paced Anypoint Platform Development: Fundamentals (Mule 4) course includes two free MuleSoft Certified Developer - Level 1 (Mule 4) certification exam attempts which you'll find in your learner dashboard.

Learn more in this help article.

We offer private classes for all of our courses. Browse the full course catalog and submit a request to MuleSoft Training Support Operations.

The answers to many frequently asked questions can be found in our help center. If you're unable to find an answer, you can submit a request.

There are multiple resources for developers, including:

Tutorials: Step-by-step guides and tutorials to solve common integration patterns with Anypoint Platform.

Documentation: In-depth references, release notes, and step-by-step tips.

MuleSoft Community: Provides local Meetup groups, leadership programs, and interactive forums.
